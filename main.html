    • Three engineering students—Rancho, Farhan, and Raju—form a deep friendship at India's top college, Imperial College of Engineering, where Rancho's innovative and passionate approach to learning challenges the rigid, exam-focused system. The film explores themes of friendship, following one's passion, and the dangers of an unhealthy academic environment, highlighted by the suicide of a fellow student due to pressure. Ultimately, the story emphasizes that true education is about knowledge and personal growth, not just grades or certificates.
    Summary : The film 3 Idiots features Aamir Khan, R. Madhavan, and Sharman Joshi in the lead roles, portraying the characters Rancho, Farhan, and Raju, respectively. The movie, released in 2009, is celebrated for its inspirational dialogues and themes of friendship and following one's passion.
